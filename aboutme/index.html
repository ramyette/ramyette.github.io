About Me

I'm Rivers Amyette. As I mentioned on the home page, I'm a sophomore in the University of South Carolina Honors College studying Computer Science.

I'm from Greenville, SC, and I first picked up a programming book (how to write html) when I was 8 years old. I made a few websites for fun, but I really got into programming when I started learning C++ in middle school. After taking both AP Computer Science Principles and AP Computer Science A in high school, I decided to pursue computer science in college.

I also participate in the Carolina Band Drumline, where I play quads. I was always in band through school, and I played the drums growing up, so joining the drumline was a great way to continue that passion in college while also meeting new people.

Recently, I decided to lock in on game development as my main focus for projects. I enjoy the process of creating interactive experiences, and it allows me to combine my childhood passion for gaming with my technical skills. I want to create the new generation of games that I would have loved to play growing up.

Recently, I've started posting YouTube videos about my development process and projects ( youtube.com/ramyette). Check that out for a more personal look into my programming journey!
